Version 1 - January 2017

Journeys

  • Direct Debit (PDF)
  • Card payment (PDF)

What we tested

  • Journey for a person buying a prescription prepayment.
  • Payment by card or Direct Debit and ending with instant digital evidence.
  • Prototype is not explicit about not receiving a card. This is so we can guage users' understanding and their reaction.

What we found

  • Users need clear payment options to make an informed choice.
  • Users need help choosing a start date that is right for them.
  • Content is not direct enough for users and leaves them with questions.
  • Users still expect to receive a card.

What we changed

  • Asking for only a postcode is strange for users.
  • Clearer information about choosing a start date.
  • Clearer information about why we want email address and telephone number.
  • Using address as a back-up for users who don't have access to mobile and email.
  • Move from 'How do you want to pay?' to 'How long do you want to cover...'
  • Clearer done page.
  • Content changes throughout.
  • '...we no longer provide plastic cards.'
  • 'NHS' moved to end of search link.
  • Print and download options improved.
